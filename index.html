About Me

Chayma Chiha

I am a serious, ambitious, and creative person, passionate about web development. As a beginner software engineer, I am always ready to take on new challenges to improve my skills, enhance myself, and elevate those around me.

I graduated with a degree in Computer Engineering, specializing in Software Engineering, from Polytech Private Higher Polytechnic School in Monastir, Tunisia (2020 – 2023). Prior to that, I completed a Technological Preparatory Cycle specializing in Physics and Chemistry at the same institution (2018 – 2020) and earned my Technical Science Baccalaureate from Lycée Ibn Jazzar, Kairouan (2017 – 2018).

My professional experience includes working as a software engineer at Etic Europe Développement where I developed Excel macros in VBA for task automation, updated desktop applications to .NET 6 with C#, and modeled labels using C# and .NET 4. Additionally, I completed an internship at KsarSoft where I worked on public transportation analysis and development in the Greater Tunis area using QGIS with Python and a web application using Django/React.
